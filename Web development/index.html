This is an organiztion that is promoting all the three elements of human life to all africans, that is language, culture and religion. We are promoting all these elements to all african to form a homogeneous society that is united with one culture, language and religion. These are kiswahili as a language, fusion of multiple cultures to form one with a single writing system eithe the amharic or we kikuyu's create one and lastly we promote an african religion that are both done and celebrated on the same day. The hardest phase is of religion in these times, Luckily the white man's religion that is christianity is shrinking and we want the african religion to leverage on these changing circumstances. Our main challenge will be Islam which seems to be on the rise as we speak in africa but all in all we have a mission and a clear vision that we are loooking foward to work on and as the saying goes good things come to those who who wait.
